A constant worry  
and fantasy flights  
foggy grey  
piercing light  
snug red sky  
speed thrill  
and drumming heart.  
Set in your ways  
well, break that set  
The road is long  
shrubs and winds  
free yourself  
my shackled son  
walk the way  
you walk the way
